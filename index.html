About Me

Welcome! I am an undergraduate student currently pursuing a B.A. in Journalism with concentrations in Reporting and Strategic Communication at the UW-Madison School of Journalism & Mass Communication. I am working to obtain certificates in Business and Digital Studies, as well.

Recent works can be found in publications such as the New York Post, including Page Six, and the Daily Cardinal across digital and print platforms. Reporting experience includes metro, crime, political and legal coverage. As news manager, staff writer and former campus news editor at the Daily Cardinal, I assist with the coordination of campus, college, city, state and associate news coverage. Additional experiences in communication include public relations, promotion and entertainment marketing.

To date, my greatest possession in life is a regular byline — a chance to be the eyes, ears and hopefully clear and unfiltered voice of many communities. I strive to communicate in an unbiased and approachable manner, dissecting both micro- and macro-level stories in an effort to find and chronicle humanity in the face of adversity.
